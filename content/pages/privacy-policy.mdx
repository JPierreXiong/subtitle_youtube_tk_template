Effective Date: December 24, 2025

At SubtitleTK (accessible via https://www.subtitletk.app), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered subtitle extraction and translation services.

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide our services effectively, we collect the following types of information:

- **Account Information**: When you register, we collect your email address and basic profile details to manage your credit balance and task history.

- **Usage Data**: We log the YouTube and TikTok URLs you submit for processing to facilitate the extraction and translation service.

- **Payment Data**: All financial transactions are handled by secure third-party payment processors. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers.

- **Cookies and Tracking**: We use cookies to maintain your session, remember your preferences, and analyze site traffic to improve user experience.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

- **Service Delivery**: To process your subtitle extractions, AI translations, and video downloads.

- **Credit Management**: To accurately deduct 1 Credit per successful task and provide automatic refunds for failed attempts.

- **Customer Support**: To troubleshoot issues with specific video links or account balances.

- **Security**: To prevent fraudulent activity, such as unauthorized scraping or credit system manipulation.

## 3. Data Retention and Storage

- **Task History**: We store your processed subtitle metadata and SRT files in secure cloud storage so you can access them via your Activity dashboard.

- **Security Measures**: We use industry-standard encryption and unique file identifiers (UUIDs) to ensure that your processed content remains private and inaccessible to unauthorized parties.

## 4. Third-Party Services

SubtitleTK utilizes trusted third-party providers to enhance our capabilities:

- **AI Models**: We use advanced AI engines for subtitle translation; however, no personally identifiable information is sent to these models.

- **Storage**: Files are stored on secure S3/R2 storage buckets with strict access controls.

- **Analytics**: We may use tools like Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our toolkit.

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on your location (such as the EU under GDPR), you may have the following rights:

- **Access**: The right to request a copy of the data we hold about you.

- **Deletion**: The right to request that we delete your account and associated task history.

- **Correction**: The right to update inaccurate or incomplete account information.

## 6. Children's Privacy

SubtitleTK does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately for removal.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date." Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of these changes.

## 8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please reach out to our team at: support@subtitletk.app.
